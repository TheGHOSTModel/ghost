Why GHOST exists

Established frameworks surface vulnerabilities in code — they ask: what is broken? In gaming platforms, the most dangerous threats need nothing broken. A grooming attack, a coordinated load, an impersonation — all use the platform exactly as designed.

GHOST asks a different question: what if nothing needs to be broken for an attack to succeed?

Beyond gaming GHOST was built for games — but the invariants generalise anywhere users interact, trade, and build trust with strangers under platform rules. The same five domains appear in every digital social system.
